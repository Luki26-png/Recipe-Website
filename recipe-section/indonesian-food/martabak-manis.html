How to Make Indonesian Martabak Manis

Martabak Manis, literally translating to "sweet pancake" in Indonesian, is a decadent and visually striking dessert. Unlike its thin and fluffy Western counterparts, Martabak Manis boasts a thick and chewy texture, achieved through a unique batter incorporating wheat flour, tapioca flour, and eggs. This base is then generously filled with a variety of sweet and savory ingredients, creating a delightful explosion of flavors and textures in every bite. Popular fillings include a classic combination of crushed peanuts and chocolate sprinkles, grated cheddar cheese for a salty-sweet contrast, or even a combination of both. The magic doesn't stop there. Before cooking, the entire concoction is liberally slathered with butter, ensuring a rich and crispy exterior. Martabak Manis is then cooked on a flat griddle, allowing the insides to cook through while the outside caramelizes for an irresistible golden-brown finish.

This delightful treat is a true crowd-pleaser, perfect for satisfying any sweet tooth. Served piping hot and sliced into wedges, Martabak Manis is meant to be shared. Each bite offers a symphony of textures, from the chewy pancake base to the contrasting crunch of the peanuts and sprinkles. The sweetness of the chocolate combined with the savory richness of the cheese (if included) creates a flavor profile that is both familiar and exciting. Enjoyed with a cup of tea or coffee, Martabak Manis is a perfect ending to a meal or a delightful indulgence on its own.

Ingredients :

  • All-Purpose Flour: Provides the base structure for the pancake.
  • Tapioca Flour: This key ingredient adds chewiness and elasticity to the pancake texture.
  • Eggs: Binds the ingredients together and contributes to the rise of the pancake.
  • Sugar: Sweetens the pancake base.
  • Milk or Water: The liquid component that hydrates the dry ingredients and creates a smooth batter.
  • Salt: Enhances the overall flavor.
  • **(Optional)** Baking Powder or Baking Soda: A small amount can be added for a slightly fluffier pancake.
  • Crushed Peanuts: A classic and popular filling that adds nuttiness and texture.
  • Chocolate Sprinkles: Sweet and colorful, these add a fun and festive touch.
  • Grated Cheddar Cheese: Offers a surprising salty-sweet contrast.
  • Condensed Milk: Drizzled on top after cooking for added sweetness and richness.
  • **(Optional)** Other Fillings: You can experiment with other options like sliced banana, Nutella, or even shredded coconut.
  • Butter: Used for greasing the griddle and adding richness to the pancake.

Instructions :

  • Whisk Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together all-purpose flour, tapioca flour, sugar, and salt (and baking powder/soda if using).
  • Combine Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs and milk or water. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, whisking until smooth. The batter should be slightly thick but pourable.
  • Choose your Fillings: Prepare your chosen fillings, such as crushing peanuts and mixing them with chocolate sprinkles, or grating cheddar cheese.
  • Heat a Griddle: Preheat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat. Grease lightly with butter.
  • Pour the Batter: Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot griddle, swirling it slightly to form a round shape.
  • Add Fillings: Sprinkle your chosen fillings generously over the uncooked pancake.
  • Fold and Cook: Once the edges start to set and the bottom begins to brown, fold the pancake in half using a spatula. Drizzle melted butter over the top. Continue cooking for a few minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Cut and Serve: Transfer the cooked Martabak Manis to a plate and cut into wedges. Drizzle with condensed milk and enjoy hot.
  • Optional Toppings: You can offer additional toppings on the side like chopped peanuts, chocolate sprinkles, or even sliced bananas for extra indulgence.
Martabak Manis truly shines when served in a social setting. Its large size and decadent nature make it perfect for sharing with friends and family. The act of watching it being cooked on a griddle, the anticipation building as the aroma fills the air, creates a sense of excitement. Once cooked, the Martabak Manis is presented in all its glory, a golden-brown masterpiece sliced into wedges, ready to be devoured. The communal aspect comes in when everyone gathers around to grab a piece, the act of sharing fostering connection and conversation. Each bite is a delightful surprise, the combination of textures and flavors offering a unique experience with every mouthful. The contrasting elements - the chewy pancake, the sweet and salty fillings, the creamy drizzle of condensed milk - all come together to create a symphony that's best enjoyed with loved ones.

Martabak Manis' versatility makes it a perfect treat throughout the day. As an afternoon snack, it provides a satisfying energy boost with its combination of carbohydrates and sweetness. Late-night cravings are easily conquered with a warm and comforting serving of Martabak Manis. But perhaps the most popular time to enjoy it is after dinner. The rich and decadent flavors serve as a perfect ending to a savory meal, offering a sweet and celebratory note to conclude the day.
